# 1. Executive Vision

## 1.1 Brand Vision

ORIGONAE is a high-margin luxury haircare brand rooted in broader African heritage.
It merges:

* Ritualistic beauty
* Spiritual grounding
* Alternative fashion edge
* Luxury minimalism
* Earth-toned aesthetics

ORIGONAE is not just a product brand — it is a **modern ancestral ritual system**.

The website must:

* Feel immersive, editorial, sacred, and intentional.
* Convey luxury without excess.
* Support global scaling.
* Support both online customers and salon partners.
* Be optimized for storytelling, conversion, and international growth.

# 7. Functional Requirements

## 7.1 Homepage

Must include:

* Cinematic hero section (video or immersive image)
* Brand manifesto section
* Featured ritual collection
* Ingredient spotlight
* Editorial storytelling block
* Salon partnership CTA
* Newsletter capture

Performance priority: LCP under 2.5s globally.

---

## 7.2 Product Detail Page (PDP)

Each PDP must include:

* Product title
* Ritual name
* Price
* High-quality images
* Ingredient breakdown
* Ritual usage guide
* Cultural inspiration
* Customer reviews
* Related ritual recommendations

Conversion features:

* Sticky Add-to-Cart
* Urgency messaging (low stock optional)
* Installment payment support (future-ready)

---

## 7.3 Checkout

Requirements:

* Guest checkout enabled
* Express pay options
* Currency auto-detection
* Tax calculation system
* Address validation
* Abandoned cart tracking

---

## 7.4 Salon Portal (Phase 1 Light)

* Dedicated Salon page
* Application form
* Admin review process
* Manual approval system
* Wholesale pricing tier logic (backend controlled)
