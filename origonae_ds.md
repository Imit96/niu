# COSMETICS PROJECT — DESIGN SYSTEM
Version: 1.0  
Scope: Website, E-commerce, Salon Touchpoints, Packaging, Content, International Scaling  
Brand Positioning: Luxury Ritual Haircare Rooted in Broader African Heritage  

---

# 1. DESIGN PRINCIPLES

## 1.1 Core Philosophy
This brand must feel:
- Spiritual but modern
- Earthy but luxurious
- Minimal but expressive
- Alternative but refined
- International but culturally grounded

The design language must communicate:
Ritual • Texture • Warmth • Depth • Confidence • Fashion-forward edge

## 1.2 Visual Keywords
Earth • Clay • Smoke • Bronze • Stone • Linen • Sand • Charcoal • Oil sheen • Sacred geometry

---

## 2.3 Color Rules
- Avoid bright or neon colors.
- No pure white backgrounds.
- Use bronze sparingly for premium effect.
- Background hierarchy must feel layered, not flat.

---

# 3. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

## 3.1 Font Pairing

### Primary Serif (Luxury + Ritual)
Use: Display headings  
Recommended: 
- Cormorant Garamond
- Playfair Display
- Or high-end serif alternative

Style:
- High contrast
- Slightly dramatic
- Spacious letter spacing

### Secondary Sans (Modern + Clean)
Use: Body, UI, buttons  
Recommended:
- Inter
- Neue Haas Grotesk
- Satoshi

Style:
- Clean
- Slightly tight tracking
- Neutral tone

---

## 3.2 Typography Scale

| Role | Size | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| H1 | 56–64px | 500 |
| H2 | 40–48px | 500 |
| H3 | 28–32px | 500 |
| Body Large | 18px | 400 |
| Body | 16px | 400 |
| Small | 14px | 400 |
| Caption | 12px | 400 |

Line-height: 120% headings, 150% body  
Letter spacing: Slight positive tracking on headings

# 5. LAYOUT SYSTEM

## 5.1 Container Width
Max width: 1200px  
Wide sections: 1440px  

## 5.2 Grid
Desktop: 12 column grid  
Tablet: 8 column  
Mobile: 4 column  

Gutter: 24px desktop  

## 5.3 Section Structure
Each section should follow:

1. Ritual Heading
2. Subheading (emotional)
3. Visual anchor (image/video/texture)
4. Content block
5. CTA

---

# 6. COMPONENT SYSTEM

## 6.1 Buttons

Primary Button:
- Background: Deep Earth
- Text: Cream
- Border radius: 4px
- Padding: 14px 24px
- Hover: Darker earth + subtle shadow

Secondary Button:
- Outline bronze
- Transparent background
- Hover: Fill bronze, text cream

No overly rounded pill buttons.

---

## 6.2 Cards (Product Cards)

- Background: Stone or Sand
- Subtle 1px border (Ash tone)
- 12–16px padding
- Large product image
- Minimal text
- Hover: Slight scale (1.02) + soft shadow

---

## 6.3 Forms

Inputs:
- Soft stone background
- 1px ash border
- No heavy shadows
- Focus state: Bronze border

Labels:
- Uppercase
- Small
- Tracking +1

---

## 6.4 Navigation

Style:
- Minimal
- Earth or transparent
- Subtle underline hover
- Avoid heavy dropdown animations

Mobile:
- Slide-in panel
- Soft sand background

---

# 7. ICON SYSTEM

Style:
- Thin line icons
- Slightly organic curves
- No cartoon style

Recommended libraries:
- Lucide
- Phosphor (thin)
- Custom SVG for ritual symbols

Icons must not feel tech-startup-like.

---

# 8. IMAGERY SYSTEM

## 8.1 Photography Direction

Lighting:
- Warm
- Natural sunlight
- Golden hour tones

Textures:
- Skin
- Fabric
- Clay
- Oil shine
- Natural hair texture

Avoid:
- Clinical white studio lighting
- Overexposed influencer style

## 8.2 Model Direction
- Diverse African heritage representation
- Strong cheekbones
- Natural hair states
- Editorial fashion influence

Energy:
Calm. Powerful. Spiritual. Intentional.

---

# 9. MOTION SYSTEM

Motion should feel:
- Slow
- Intentional
- Smooth
- Cinematic

Use:
- Fade in
- Slide up 10–20px
- 300–500ms duration
- Ease-in-out curves

Avoid:
- Bounce
- Fast elastic animations
- Playful micro-interactions

---

# 10. PACKAGING DESIGN SYSTEM

## 10.1 Material Feel
- Matte bottles
- Soft-touch finish
- Embossed logo
- Bronze foil accent

## 10.2 Label Rules
- Minimal text
- Serif headline
- Clear product category
- Ritual instructions section

Tone of copy:
Ritualistic, poetic, grounded.

---

# 11. CONTENT DESIGN SYSTEM

Voice:
- Confident
- Sensory
- Grounded
- Elevated
- Not corporate

Sentence Structure:
Short statements.
Intentional pacing.
Emotional but controlled.

Avoid:
- Salesy hype language
- Excess exclamation marks

---

# 12. E-COMMERCE UX PRINCIPLES

Product page must include:
- Ritual description
- Ingredients
- Texture description
- Who it's for
- How to apply
- Lifestyle photography
- Review section
- Related ritual products

Checkout:
- Minimal steps
- Guest checkout enabled
- Clean distraction-free design

---

# 13. ACCESSIBILITY

- Minimum contrast ratio WCAG AA
- Alt text for all imagery
- Focus states visible
- Touch targets 44px minimum

---

# 14. INTERNATIONAL SCALING CONSIDERATIONS

- Multi-currency ready
- Multi-language ready
- Culturally neutral but rooted visuals
- Avoid region-specific slang
- Global shipping UX clarity

---

# 15. DO NOTs

- No bright gradients
- No startup SaaS aesthetic
- No excessive rounded UI
- No glossy plastic look
- No loud typography stacking

---

# 16. FUTURE EXTENSIONS

Design system must scale to:
- Salon interiors
- Pop-up installations
- Campaign microsites
- Mobile app
- Packaging line expansion

Consistency > Trend.
